replicate.fun/music

What do you want the music to do to you? How can you do that in one session?

Process

  1. Add atmosphere - focus on sound design; establishes key, long notes
  2. Add bassline - focus on rhithm; establishes tempo, long pattern
  3. Add drums - focus on clarity; establishes cohesion,
  4. Add melody - focus on melody; establishes interest

Minimal effort, maximal reward

  • Few sounds, yet complex
  • Funky bass rhythms
  • Delay & Reverb
  • Arpegiators
  • Lots of bends
  • Trance gates

Philosophies

  • Tracks are independently interesting
  • Minimal effort, maximal reward
  • Midas Touch; only add it if its gold
  • Iterate; start simple and evolve (sound design, melodies, rhithms)
  • Conciously optimize; know what you're evoking - eg. dance, cry, relax, forget, headbang, energize, think, etc

Frameworks

  • KSSS (keep shit simple stupid) - 4 instrument songs
  • EVEV (environment, vitals, emotion, voice) - reformat stories for standardization
  • Simple Simon (atmosphere, drums, bassline, melody) - EVEV mapped to music while you KSSS
